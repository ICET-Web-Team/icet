Our values

Founder's Message

Welcome to the iCET community!

As the founder of iCET, it is my privilege to introduce you to the exciting world of software engineering. Understanding the core concepts of software engineering isn't just an advantage; it's a necessity. At iCET, we've meticulously designed programs to equip you with a solid foundation in software engineering. Whether you're seeking higher education, working on personal projects, or considering a career in IT, we provide you with the knowledge and skills you need to succeed. Our aim is to empower you to understand the technology that shapes our world. Knowledge is the key to unlocking your potential and seizing opportunities in our evolving digital landscape. I encourage you to make the most of your time at iCET. Embrace learning, ask questions, and delve into the diverse facets of software engineering. The skills you acquire here will serve you well in your academic and professional journeys and open doors to exciting career prospects. I'm eager to see how you'll apply the knowledge gained here to excel in your educational and professional ventures. Remember, you're not just learning; you're preparing for a future where technology plays an ever-increasing role. Welcome to iCET, and welcome to a world of endless possibilities.

Sincerely,
Dr. Niroth Samarawickrama
Founder, iCET

CEO's Message

I am truly delighted to welcome you on board for this exciting journey.

I am truly delighted to welcome you on board for this exciting journey. We've crafted programs that empowers you to delve into the very core of software engineering. Our goal is simple yet profound: to provide you with a strong foundation that will serve as a springboard for your future endeavors. In the world of software engineering, creativity meets logic, and innovation knows no bounds. We encourage you to embrace this opportunity fully. Each lesson, every assignment, and all the practical projects are stepping stones to your growth. Use them to develop your problem-solving abilities, analytical thinking, and your capacity to adapt to an ever-changing technological landscape. Remember, our focus is not just about coursework; it's about cultivating a mindset of curiosity and a passion for excellence. The skills you acquire here will not only help you excel in your academic pursuits but will also prepare you for success in the broader software engineering ecosystem. Your journey with us is a beginning, not an end. We have no doubt that you will carry the knowledge and the skill set gained here into your future endeavors, whether it be higher studies, free lance projects, or professional pursuits. Welcome to the community. Embrace this journey, learn voraciously, and dare to dream big.

Warm Regards,
Samila Senanayaka
